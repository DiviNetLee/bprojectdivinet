What Is A Web Designer?

Photo by Aral Tasher on Unsplash


First things first, A Web Designer is someone who prepares content for the Web. This role is mainly related to the styling and layout of pages with content, including text and images.Web designers use many technologies but commonly rely on hypertext and hypermedia resources including HTML, CSS and additional Web design tools.

That is what I do. I work on what your customers or clients will see when they go to your website.


Having a website may not seem like something you think you need, maybe your think "But Tyler my business is doing great without the help of a web designer," or "with the one I had made for me 9 years ago. Why on earth would I need to change that?" Well fictional person I just created, I'll tell you why. Consider this, you probably have a car. You put gas in that car. You don't technically need your car, but it makes your life easier and run smoother, so you keep your car. Well having a website is the same. Although you may be able to get by without it, it will help your life run smoother and put more money in your bank. So do you want to really be without a web designer on your side?
